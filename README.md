## 📝 About

Built for the hackathon, **MicroHedge** bridges the gap between traditional commodity markets and blockchain. It allows users to **create hedge positions for commodities** like coffee and corn, locking in prices to secure income and reduce risk — all on the **Injective blockchain**.

---

## ⚠️ Problem

In emerging markets:

* Coffee prices drop → farmers lose income  
* Corn prices fluctuate → small businesses struggle to plan  
* Traditional hedging tools exist but are **complex, costly, and inaccessible**

---

## 💡 Solution

**MicroHedge leverages Injective** to:

* Enable **anyone** to hedge commodity prices without intermediaries  
* Make hedging **affordable, transparent, and trustless**  
* Provide **real-time tracking** of hedge positions  
* Offer **a human-readable dashboard** for users to monitor their positions

---

## ✨ Features

* **Single-click hedge creation** — Quickly lock in commodity prices.  
* **On-chain transparency with Injective** — All positions are verifiable.  
* **Off-chain accounts** — Auto-generated usernames linked to wallet addresses for easy onboarding.  
* **Real-time tracking** — Monitor hedges and settlement status effortlessly.  
* **Hackathon-ready UI** — Simple, intuitive, and visually appealing dashboard.
